Understanding the Congestion Map

Unlike the standard hotspot map, this thermal heatmap emphasizes structural traffic congestion and gridlock risk based on the Congestion Risk Index (CRI). Bright red and orange spots represent areas where violations cause severe ripple effects across the traffic network.
